Areas of Expertise

Areas we operate and dominate

Through knowledge in the area, Eduardo Mayr Advogados is able to defend the interests of its clients in the areas of crimes against the national financial system, economic order, money laundering, environment, public administration, bidding processes, Jury Court, Audit of Justice Military, Superior Courts, among countless others foreseen in our legislation.

Our performance is quite comprehensive in this field of law, always with a view to avoiding the much-feared labor risk which can lead to significant and expressive financial losses, providing a secure and personalized support in the business management of this risk, through opinions, face-to-face meetings or by video and lectures, consultation of individual or collective rights, analysis or elaboration of: contracts, agreements, drafts of collective or individual norms, negotiations of Collective Agreements and Collective Bargaining Agreements, with Unions.

The Firm offers extensive experience in this important branch of business law to its clients, participating in negotiations of Economic Activities (goodwill), real estate and patrimonial assets in their purchase, sale or assignment, protection, in litigation in the State and Courts of Justice Special Civil, whether filing lawsuits, or acting in their defenses, in the most diverse branches of this law, such as in enforcement actions, leases, indemnities originating in breach of contract, traffic accidents and others, bankruptcy applications, legal disputes of the consumer, analyzing or drawing up contracts of a civil nature, advising on legal transactions of interest to its clients, aiming with the necessary security, to neutralize risks.

In these areas, we initially act with guidance and mediation between those involved, with the aim of objectifying consensual processes, in order to avoid litigation in the judicial sphere. However, as the conciliatory attempts are unsuccessful, we still offer representation in legal proceedings aimed at the family and Childhood and Youth and Elderly segment, in all situations competent to these branches. We also work in the orphanological area, opening Inventories, Wills and drawing up of Sharing Deeds, either judicially or extrajudicially.
